# UC-BETA-012 — Sector ETF Rotation (Tactical)

**Timeframe:** 1–6 months

## Thesis

A tactical overlay on top of the core diversified portfolio, rotating capital into sector-specific exchange-traded funds when the broader macroeconomic regime favors a particular sector — for example, energy or materials during a commodity-driven inflationary period, or financials during a rising rate environment. This is explicitly a satellite position layered around the core holdings, not a replacement for them, and is sized and time-bound accordingly.

## Entry Signal

A real, identifiable macroeconomic regime supporting a specific sector must be present, assessed through interest rate trends, commodity price direction, and broader economic cycle positioning. The sector's relative strength against the broader market should be confirmed as genuinely positive and turning upward, not simply assumed from the macro thesis alone. Position size for any single tactical sector bet is kept deliberately small relative to the overall portfolio, with a limit on how many tactical sector positions are held simultaneously.

## Exit Rules

The position is exited when the macroeconomic regime that justified the trade shifts — this is a regime-based exit, not a pure price-based one, since the entire premise of the trade was the macro backdrop rather than the technical setup alone. A sector showing extended, overbought conditions on longer timeframes is also treated as a signal to begin reducing the tactical position.
