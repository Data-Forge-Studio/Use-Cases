# UC-BETA-011 — Broad Market ETF (Core Portfolio)

**Timeframe:** Indefinite

## Thesis

Systematic, recurring contributions to broad, diversified market index funds form the foundational core of long-term portfolio construction. This is intentionally not a trading strategy — it is a disciplined savings and compounding approach that captures broad market returns over time without attempting to identify individual winners or time entry points, recognizing that consistent, low-cost, diversified exposure has historically outperformed most attempts at active stock selection over long horizons.

## Entry Signal

Contributions occur automatically on a fixed monthly schedule, entirely independent of market conditions or short-term price movement. During major market corrections — declines of 15% or more — additional, opportunistic contributions beyond the regular schedule may be made, since broad market drawdowns have historically represented favorable long-term entry points for diversified index exposure.

## Exit Rules

There is no price target and no planned exit. These holdings are intended to compound over a full investing lifetime. Adjustments are limited to periodic rebalancing to maintain target allocations, with meaningful drawdown of the position reserved for actual retirement income needs decades into the future rather than any tactical market view.
