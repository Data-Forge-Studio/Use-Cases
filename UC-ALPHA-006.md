# UC-ALPHA-006 — High Volume Momentum Breakout

**Timeframe:** 1 day to 2 weeks

## Thesis

Volume frequently leads price. An asset breaking out of a multi-week base on exceptionally high trading volume — well beyond what recent history would suggest as normal — is read as a signal that informed or large-scale capital is entering the position ahead of, or in direct response to, a meaningful catalyst. The thesis treats volume itself as the primary signal, with price action serving as confirmation rather than the sole driver of the decision.

## Entry Signal

Trading volume at least three times the 30-day average, occurring concurrently with price breaking through a resistance level that has held for multiple weeks. The relative strength indicator should be accelerating upward from a moderate reading (below 60) rather than already extended, and there should be minimal overhead resistance for at least 10% above the breakout point — a genuinely clear path for the move to continue.

## Exit Rules

A meaningful fade in volume on subsequent candles is treated as an early warning that the move may be losing conviction. The hard exit trigger is price closing back below the original breakout level, which would indicate the breakout was a false signal rather than the start of a genuine trend.
