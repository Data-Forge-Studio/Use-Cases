# UC-BETA-013 — US Market ETF (Long Term Core)

**Timeframe:** Indefinite

## Thesis

Broad United States market exposure through currency-hedged exchange-traded funds, complementing domestic Canadian holdings with exposure to the considerably larger and more diversified US equity market. Currency hedging is used to isolate the underlying equity return from currency fluctuation, which can otherwise meaningfully distort returns over shorter holding periods, though it introduces its own modest cost.

## Entry Signal

Systematic monthly contributions, consistent with the broad market ETF approach. Meaningful market corrections of 15% or more from recent highs are treated as opportunities for additional contributions beyond the regular schedule.

## Exit Rules

No price target and no planned exit — these are long-term core holdings. Rebalancing occurs periodically to maintain intended allocation weights between domestic and international exposure.
