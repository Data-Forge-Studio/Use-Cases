# UC-ALPHA-001 — Wave 2 Meme Coin Swing (DEX/Solana)

**Timeframe:** Hours to 3 days

## Thesis

A meme coin that had an initial pump (Wave 1) is now consolidating above its launch level, setting up for a second leg higher. The thesis is that early speculative interest establishes a price floor, and a second wave of attention — driven by social momentum, exchange listings, or influencer coverage — can push price beyond the Wave 1 high. Entry is deliberately timed for after the first pullback, not the initial launch spike, because Wave 1 entries carry the worst risk/reward in this category: maximum hype, maximum slippage, and the highest probability of a reversal.

This is a high-velocity, high-risk category by design. Meme coins on decentralized exchanges have no fundamental backing, no revenue, and no long-term value proposition beyond attention and liquidity. The entire trade is built on momentum and crowd psychology, not asset quality.

## Entry Signal

- Wave 1 confirmed: an initial pump and at least one meaningful pullback have already occurred
- Price holding above the original launch level on the pullback (a failure to hold suggests Wave 1 was the only move this asset had)
- Volume contracting into the consolidation phase — a sign of exhausted sellers rather than panic
- Social momentum indicators still present (mentions, engagement, community activity not yet faded)
- Contract security check passed — no obvious rug-pull mechanisms, ownership renounced or reasonably distributed, liquidity locked where verifiable

## Exit Rules

- Hard profit target set at or near the Wave 1 high — this is the natural resistance level and the most likely point for a second wave to stall
- Stop placed below the consolidation low — if this level breaks, the Wave 2 thesis has failed
- Time stop: if no meaningful price movement within 24 hours of entry, exit regardless of price — stagnation in this asset class usually precedes a slow bleed, not a delayed breakout

## What This Is Not

Do not chase the initial Wave 1 pump. The risk/reward on a fresh launch is structurally unfavorable — you are buying into euphoria with no price discovery yet established. This use case exists specifically to avoid that mistake by waiting for the market to show its hand first.
