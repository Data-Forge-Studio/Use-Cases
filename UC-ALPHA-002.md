# UC-ALPHA-002 — Large Cap Crypto Long Term Hold

**Timeframe:** Indefinite

## Thesis

Quality large cap cryptocurrencies — those ranked among the top tier by market capitalization, with real network usage, established developer activity, and a multi-year track record — are treated as long-term positions rather than trades. The thesis rests on the view that blockchain networks with genuine utility and network effects compound in value over multi-year cycles, similar in spirit to holding a quality equity through business cycles. This is a conviction position, not a tactical trade, and is held through the inevitable 40-70% drawdowns that characterize even the strongest crypto assets during bear phases.

## Entry Signal

Systematic monthly dollar-cost averaging into the position, independent of price. Additional, opportunistic buying is layered in during periods of extreme market fear, when sentiment indicators show capitulation-level pessimism. No technical trigger is required for the recurring DCA leg — the discipline of the schedule is the entry signal.

## Exit Rules

No stop loss is used on this position — by design, since the entire thesis depends on holding through volatility that would stop out a tactical trader. Reduction (never full exit) is considered only at genuine cycle-top indicators: sustained extreme greed readings over weeks rather than days, parabolic price extension well beyond historical cycle patterns, and mainstream media saturation suggesting speculative excess. Even then, only a partial trim is taken — the core position is intended to be held across multiple cycles.

## What This Is Not

Do not confuse this with active trading. This position is explicitly designed to be held through 50%+ peak-to-trough declines without panic selling. If the discipline to hold through such declines is not present, this use case is being misapplied.
