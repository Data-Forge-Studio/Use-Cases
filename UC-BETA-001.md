# UC-BETA-001 — TSX Blue Chip Long Term Buy & Hold

**Timeframe:** Indefinite

## Thesis

High-quality, large-capitalization companies listed on the Toronto Stock Exchange with durable competitive positions, consistent dividend histories, and strong balance sheets form the core of a long-term portfolio. The thesis is straightforward compounding: businesses with sustainable competitive advantages and disciplined capital allocation tend to grow shareholder value over long horizons, and the dividend income provides a real, tangible return independent of price appreciation. This is the foundational, lowest-turnover category in the overall strategy.

## Entry Signal

Candidates are screened for a dividend yield above 3%, a payout ratio below 70% (indicating the dividend is sustainable from earnings rather than financed by debt), a history of consistent or growing dividends over at least five years, and a fundamentally sound balance sheet. Entries are accumulated systematically over time rather than concentrated at a single price point, recognizing that timing a multi-decade holding precisely is neither necessary nor reliably achievable.

## Exit Rules

These positions are held indefinitely by design. Exit is triggered only by a genuine deterioration in the underlying thesis: a dividend cut (which signals real financial stress, not just a temporary earnings miss), a fundamental breakdown in the competitive position of the business, or a structural capital reallocation decision unrelated to the stock's own performance.
