# UC-ALPHA-003 — Large Cap Breakout Swing

**Timeframe:** Days to 4 weeks

## Thesis

A major, established cryptocurrency breaking above a well-defined consolidation range on meaningfully elevated trading volume, with the broader macro and crypto market regime supportive of continued upside. This is a momentum continuation trade: the thesis is that a clean technical breakout, confirmed by volume and occurring within a favorable macro backdrop, has a statistically higher probability of follow-through than a breakout occurring in isolation or against the prevailing regime.

## Entry Signal

Price closing decisively above a clearly identified resistance level on a 4-hour candle close, accompanied by trading volume at least double the recent average. Momentum indicators (RSI) should be rising but not already in deeply overbought territory, which would suggest the move is exhausted rather than beginning. The broader market regime — assessed via sentiment indicators, dominance trends, and macro liquidity conditions — should read as risk-on or neutral, not risk-off. Confirmation is sought across multiple timeframes (15-minute, 1-hour, and 4-hour) showing alignment rather than conflicting signals.

## Exit Rules

The profit target is set at the next identifiable resistance level above the breakout point, derived from prior price structure. The stop is placed below the breakout level itself on a 4-hour close basis — if price falls back below the level it just broke, the breakout has likely failed and the position is closed regardless of unrealized loss. A time-based exit applies if the position shows no meaningful progress within two weeks, since a genuine breakout should show continuation within that window.
